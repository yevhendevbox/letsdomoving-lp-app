Choose your plan

basic

  • * You take charge of packing while we handle the transportation.
  • * Ideal for price-conscious individuals who prefer a simple and straightforward move.
  • * You pack all your belongings, and we handle the safe transportation.
  • * Upon arrival at your new location, we carefully place boxes and furniture in their designated rooms.

enchanced

  • * You take care of packing small loose items, while we handle the packing of larger items and provide disassembly and wrapping services for all furniture.
  • * Upon reaching your new location, we ensure that boxes are sorted and placed in their respective rooms. Additionally, our team will assemble and set up your furniture, leaving you with a hassle-free experience. We also take care of removing any garbage or packing materials.

ultimate

  • * Sit back and relax with our turn-key solution that takes care of every aspect of your move.
  • * Leave the packing to us! Our team will handle the full packing service for all your items, ensuring their safety and security.
  • * We meticulously disassemble and wrap your furniture to protect it during the move, providing professional care every step of the way.
  • * Upon arrival at your new location, our team will diligently separate boxes and belongings into their designated rooms. We'll go the extra mile by assembling and setting up your furniture, leaving you with a perfectly organized space. Plus, we take care of removing any garbage or packing materials, ensuring a clean and tidy environment.
* Additional services such as unpacking, cleaning, floor and carpet protection can be added to any of the plans, give us a call and our Moving Specialist will be happy to discuss with you further!
